Our Story

Ernakulam Pet Hospital was founded in 2010 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a veterinarian with a dream of making pet healthcare more accessible and technologically advanced.

What started as a small clinic has grown into a comprehensive platform that combines traditional veterinary expertise with cutting-edge AI technology. Our AI skin diagnostics system, launched in 2022, has helped thousands of pet owners detect conditions early.

Today, we serve over 10,000 pet families across the country, with a team of 50+ dedicated veterinarians and support staff who share our passion for animal welfare.
